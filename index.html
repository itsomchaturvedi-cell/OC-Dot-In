01 About Me

I started freelancing at 14. It wasn't really a venture — just early hustle. My first actual company, Pepplo Softwares, came at 16. I took it from zero to a 30-person team shipping software and digital marketing for clients across India, the US, and Italy, and exited in 2025. That's where I learned to ship real products and run operations at scale — the foundation everything after it is built on.

Today I'm Managing Director at Chartered Studies (Bharatverse Edtech Pvt Ltd), an EdTech company built to fix a specific problem: professional education for CA, CS, and CMA students that is priced like a luxury when it should be a right. It started as a YouTube channel. It's now a full platform used by students across the country.

Alongside that, I've co-founded Sankalp Academy, a coaching institution for secondary school students in Kalyan, and Bullstreet Capital, a mutual fund advisory and distribution boutique. I also founded Chaturvedi Group, a holding company with two verticals — PPP Projects, and a DTDC logistics franchise where I'm a sleeping partner.

I hold a Bachelor's in Commerce from NMIMS and a Master's in Applied Economics from St. Xavier's College — mostly because I wanted to understand why some systems work and most don't. I've also written two books, because if you can't explain an idea simply, you don't understand it well enough yet.

03 My Vision

Access shouldn't be the reason someone doesn't get a good education, a good product, or a good opportunity.

Every business I build starts from that constraint, not from what's easiest to sell. That's the entire thesis behind Chartered Studies: professional education that competes on quality, not just price, in a market that has priced most students out for decades.

Long term, I want to build institutions the way Tata built his — on trust that outlasts any single product cycle — while moving with the speed and first-principles thinking of the founders I respect most. Slow and cautious rarely beats fast and honest.
